## User Experience Flow 🎬

```
1. User opens app
   ↓
2. Enters standup update
   ↓
3. Clicks "Translate"
   ↓
4. 🌀 Loader spins (1.5-2 seconds)
   ↓
5. Funny response appears with typing effect
   ↓
6. Confidence score animates (e.g., 92%)
   ↓
7. Meme image appears with title & text
   ↓
8. "🤫 Reveal Secret" button shows
   ↓
9. User clicks reveal
   ↓
10. Modal pops: "You've Been April Fooled! 🎭"
   ↓
11. Reads generic, friendly message
   ↓
12. Clicks "Good one! 😄"
   ↓
13. Modal closes
```
